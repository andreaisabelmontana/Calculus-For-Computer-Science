PROGRAM:CONT
:ClrHome
:Disp "CONTINUITY CHECK"
:Disp "AT X = A"
:Disp "F(X) IN Y1"
:Prompt A
:Y1(A-1E-5)->L
:Y1(A+1E-5)->R
:Y1(A)->V
:ClrHome
:Disp "LEFT   =",L
:Disp "RIGHT  =",R
:Disp "F(A)   =",V
:Disp ""
:If abs(L-R)>=1E-4
:Then
:Disp "JUMP DISCONT."
:Goto Z
:End
:If abs(L-V)>=1E-4
:Then
:Disp "REMOVABLE DISC."
:Disp "FILLED VALUE:"
:Disp (L+R)/2
:Goto Z
:End
:Disp "CONTINUOUS AT A"
:Lbl Z
