PROGRAM:GRAD
:ClrHome
:Disp "GRADIENT OF F"
:Disp "USE X AND Y"
:Input "F(X,Y)=",Str1
:Prompt X,Y
:X->P:Y->Q
:.001->H
:P+H->X:Q->Y:expr(Str1)->A
:P-H->X:expr(Str1)->B
:(A-B)/(2H)->U
:P->X:Q+H->Y:expr(Str1)->A
:Q-H->Y:expr(Str1)->B
:(A-B)/(2H)->V
:P->X:Q->Y:expr(Str1)->F
:{U,V}->L2
:ClrHome
:Disp "F(P,Q) =",F
:Disp "Fx =",U
:Disp "Fy =",V
:Disp "|GRAD F| =",sqrt(U^2+V^2)
:Disp "STORED IN L2"
